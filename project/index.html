Read, imagine, and connect with Huancavelica.

This website shares traditional stories and legends from Huancavelica, Peru. It is designed to help students practice reading in an engaging way while they learn about the culture and history of their own region.

Start exploring the collection of tales or choose one featured legend to read today.

How to use this website

  1. Choose a story that interests you from the Stories page.
  2. Read the text carefully and pay attention to unfamiliar words.
  3. After reading, talk with a friend or teacher about the message of the story.
  4. Return to the website and mark your favorite legends (this feature will use localStorage).
