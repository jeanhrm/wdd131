About Peru

Peru is a country on the western side of South America, bordered by the Pacific Ocean. It is known for its rich history, diverse landscapes, and vibrant cultures.

  • Capital: Lima
  • Official language: Spanish (and many indigenous languages)
  • Famous sites: Machu Picchu, the Nazca Lines, Lake Titicaca, the Amazon Rainforest
  • Highlights: Andean mountains, coastal deserts, Amazon jungle, and incredible food such as ceviche and lomo saltado.
